Cost optimization
Problems

Our company is currently experiencing a significant cost burden from our AWS cloud infrastructure, which is impacting our profitability and ability to invest in other areas of the business. Despite efforts to optimize costs, our AWS bills continue to increase, and we are concerned that we are not getting the most value from our cloud resources. We are looking for solutions to reduce our AWS costs while maintaining the performance and reliability of our cloud infrastructure.

Solutions
  • Conduct a comprehensive cost assessment and optimize AWS usage.
  • Explore the possibility of migrating workloads to other cost-effective cloud providers.
  • Review cloud architecture and identify opportunities to optimize infrastructure, processes, and operations.
  • Establish cloud governance policies and set up a cloud center of excellence (CCoE) to manage cloud operations and provide oversight.
  • Implement cost management tools to gain greater visibility into cloud costs and optimize spending.
